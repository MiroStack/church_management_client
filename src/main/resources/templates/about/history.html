History

Bible Baptist Ekklesia of Kawit

Bible Baptist Ekklesia of Kawit has a rich history of God's faithfulness and provision. Here are some key milestones in our journey:

2006
Started to have Mission work in Kawit
2015
Started to support Foreign Missionaries
2016
1st Anniversary Celebration as an Organized Church
2021
1st World Missions Conference
2025
10th Church Anniversary & 4th World Missions Conference Throughout our history, we have seen God's hand at work in our church, bringing people to salvation, growing believers in their faith, and using our congregation to impact our community and beyond with the Gospel of Jesus Christ. We look forward to what God will continue to do through Bible Baptist Ekklesia of Kawit in the years to come as we remain faithful to His Word and His mission.
