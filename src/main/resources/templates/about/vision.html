Our Vision

Bible Baptist Ekklesia of Kawit

"We are independent Bible Baptist Church fulfilling and carry-out Christ great commission to propagate the Gospel to all mankind and win souls to Christ as found in Matthew 28:19-20. Firmly preach the word of truth into the world. The bible is the final authority of our faith and practice and pure foundation of doctrines."


Our vision is to be a church that:

Glorifies God in all that we do

Reaches the lost with the Gospel of Jesus Christ

Disciples believers to spiritual maturity

Equips the saints for the work of ministry

Impacts our community with the love of Christ

Plants new churches to expand God's kingdom


We envision a church where every member is actively involved in ministry, using their spiritual gifts to serve God and others, and where lives are transformed by the power of the Gospel.
