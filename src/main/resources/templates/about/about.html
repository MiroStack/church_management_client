About Our Church

Serving Christ and the community since 1985

Our Story

Bible Baptist Ekklesia of Kawit was founded in 1985 with a vision to spread the Gospel throughout Kawit and beyond. What began as a small gathering of believers has grown into a vibrant community of faith dedicated to worship, discipleship, and service.


Through the years, our church has remained committed to the inerrant Word of God, teaching Biblical principles and helping people develop a personal relationship with Jesus Christ. We've seen countless lives transformed through the power of God's love and grace.


Today, Bible Baptist Ekklesia of Kawit continues to be a beacon of hope in our community, offering various ministries and programs designed to meet the spiritual, emotional, and physical needs of people from all walks of life.

What We Believe

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, inerrant Word of God and our final authority for faith and practice.

God

We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Jesus Christ

We believe in the deity of Jesus Christ, His virgin birth, sinless life, atoning death, bodily resurrection, and His coming return.

Salvation

We believe salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone, not by works.
